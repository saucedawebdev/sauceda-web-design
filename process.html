Step by Step

Seven clear steps from idea to launch.

No surprises, no confusion — just a straightforward path to your new website.

  1. 01

    Free Consultation

    We talk about your business, goals, services, customers, and what your website needs to accomplish.

  2. 02

    Free Homepage Mockup

    I create a custom homepage concept so you can see the direction before committing to the full website.

  3. 03

    Design Approval

    We review the mockup together, make adjustments, and lock in the visual direction.

  4. 04

    Website Development

    I build the website with clean code, responsive layouts, fast performance, and modern design.

  5. 05

    Revisions

    You review the website and request changes so the final version matches your business and goals.

  6. 06

    Launch

    I help connect the domain, hosting, forms, and final settings so your website is ready to go live.

  7. 07

    Ongoing Support

    After launch, I can help with updates, fixes, maintenance, and future improvements.
