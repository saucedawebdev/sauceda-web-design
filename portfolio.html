Elite Home Services

Elite Home

Project Overview

Elite Home Services needed a website that matched the quality of their work. They had an outdated site that wasn't mobile-friendly and generated almost no online leads.

The Challenge

Their old website looked unprofessional, loaded slowly on mobile, and had no clear path for customers to request a quote. They were losing business to competitors with better online presence.

The Solution

I designed and built a clean, modern website with prominent call-to-action buttons, a mobile-first layout, service area pages, and trust badges — all optimized for speed and search engines.

The Outcome

A professional digital presence that builds instant credibility, works flawlessly on every device, and gives customers a clear way to get in touch — turning the website into a 24/7 lead generation tool.

  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Responsive Design
  • SEO
