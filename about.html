My Story

From the Navy to building websites for businesses that matter.

I proudly served five years in the United States Navy. That experience shaped who I am today — it taught me discipline, accountability, teamwork, and an attention to detail that I bring to every project I take on.

After leaving the military, I discovered a real passion for web development and design. I loved the idea of building something from scratch — something that could help a real business grow and succeed online.

That's why I founded Sauceda Web Design. I saw too many small businesses stuck with outdated websites that didn't reflect the quality of their work. I wanted to change that — to give local business owners access to the kind of professional online presence they deserve, without the agency-level price tag.

Today, I believe every business — no matter how small — deserves a website that reflects the quality of its work. That's not just a tagline for me. It's the reason I do what I do.

My Approach

How I work with every project.

Everything I do starts with your business — not a template, not a trend, not what looks cool. What works for you.

01

Understanding Your Business

Before I design anything, I take the time to learn about your business, your customers, and what makes you different. Your website should feel like an extension of your brand — not a generic template.

02

Designing With Your Customers in Mind

Every layout, color, and word is chosen with your ideal customer in mind. I design for the person who lands on your site and needs a reason to trust you and take action.

03

Building Responsive Experiences

Your customers are on their phones. I build every site mobile-first so it looks and works flawlessly on every device — because that's how people will actually find you.

04

Clear Communication

No jargon, no ghosting, no surprises. I keep you updated at every stage and explain everything in plain language so you're always in the loop.

05

Long-Term Relationships

I don't disappear after launch. I'm here for updates, improvements, and support whenever your business needs it — because your growth doesn't stop when the site goes live.

06

Professional Support

When you have a question or need a change, you talk directly to me — not a ticket system or an account manager. Real support from someone who knows your project inside and out.
