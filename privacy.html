Privacy Policy details

Sauceda Web Design ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

Who We Are

Sauceda Web Design is a freelance web design business operated by Ramon Sauceda, based in the Rio Grande Valley, Texas. We build websites for small businesses and local service providers.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at ramon@saucedawebdesign.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it through our contact form. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Business industry
  • Project type, estimated page count, budget, and timeline
  • Current website URL (optional)
  • Project details and goals you share in your message
  • Your consent to be contacted regarding your inquiry

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use contact form data for unrelated marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Review your project inquiry or free homepage mockup request
  • Respond to you by email regarding your website project
  • Prepare quotes, mockups, and project proposals
  • Communicate with you throughout a project, if you choose to work with us

We use your information only for legitimate business purposes related to Sauceda Web Design services.

Form Submissions Through Formspree

Our contact form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. When you submit the contact form, your information is transmitted to Formspree and then delivered to us by email.

Formspree may process and store submission data according to its own privacy practices. We encourage you to review the Formspree Privacy Policy for more information about how they handle data.

Google Maps

Our Contact page includes an embedded Google Map showing our general service area. Google may collect information about your visit — such as your IP address, device information, and interaction with the map — according to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not control Google's data collection practices. The map is provided for your convenience to understand where we are based and the regions we serve.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, provide services, maintain business records, and comply with applicable legal obligations. If you would like us to delete your information, contact us at ramon@saucedawebdesign.com.

Your Choices

You are not required to submit our contact form to browse this website. If you have submitted information and would like to request access, correction, or deletion of your data, email us and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
